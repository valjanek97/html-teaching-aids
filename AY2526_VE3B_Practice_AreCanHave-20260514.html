秘笈：點樣估 Are / Can / Have?

Cheat Sheet: How to guess the magic words

唔想溫書？字彙太難睇唔明？唔使驚！我哋教你點樣做小偵探。只要望下啲字的「形狀」同「車尾（字尾）」，就可以屈機估中！
所有答案都係用嚟接落依句度："...because they [魔法字] ______."
Too hard to read? Don't worry! Just look at the word shapes and endings to guess correctly!

ARE

讚緊件貨好正 (Description)

如果字尾係：
-able / -ible (e.g. reliable, suitable)
-ive (e.g. interactive, cost-effective)
-al / -ful (e.g. practical, useful)

👉 呢啲係 形容詞Adjectives: Words that describe things.。
請用 ARE。
HAVE

有咩配件/功能 (Things/Ideas)

結尾又長又噚氣：
-tion / -ment (e.g. installation, enhancement)
-ity / -ance (e.g. durability, disturbance)
或者字尾有 -s (e.g. issues, functions)
仲有三大神字：budget, schedule, lifespan

👉 呢啲係件貨自帶嘅 名詞Nouns: Objects, concepts, or things.。
請用 HAVE。
CAN

件貨識做咩 (Action)

短小精悍，通常喺最頭！冇上面嗰啲古怪字尾。

(e.g. draw, enhance, affect, facilitate, avoid, install, require)

👉 我哋統一幫所有 動詞Verbs: Action words. 加幫助字。
請用 CAN。

打小大佬：10個地獄級伏位！

Mini Bosses: 10 Unexpected Traps

英文點會咁簡單！總有啲字唔跟規矩，或者特登跣你。遇到呢啲「小大佬」，記住以下嘅拆解秘訣！
English always has exceptions! When you see these "Bosses", remember these tricks!

Boss 1
(-ing)

The "-ing" 偽裝者 (The Imposter)

e.g., engaging, confusing, distracting.
雖然有 -ing，但喺度係形容詞！👉 破解法：一律用 ARE！ (They are engaging)

Boss 2
(NO)

"NO" 陷阱 (The Sneaky 'No')

e.g., no multimedia functions, no technical issues.
👉 破解法：照樣用 HAVE！ "They have no functions" = 佢哋冇呢個功能。

Boss 3
(FOR)

FOR 字頭 (The Preposition Start)

e.g., for everyday classes, for school use.
👉 破解法：見到 for 喺最前，一律加 ARE！ (They are for everyday classes).

Boss 4
(-ed 形容詞)

The "-ed" 形容詞 (State, not action)

e.g., limited in features, complicated.
👉 破解法：如果佢係形容緊件貨好差/好難，都係用 ARE！ (They are limited in features).
